# Eye Map — User Guide

## About the App
Eye Map is an open-source navigation application designed specifically for people with visual impairments. The app uses voice guidance to help users navigate safely and independently.

## Main Features
- Voice-based step-by-step route guidance
- Automatic announcement of bus/tram numbers
- Real-time stop announcements
- Obstacle warnings
- Fully hands-free voice interface

## How to Use
1. Open the app
2. Say or type your destination
3. Choose the preferred route (walking or public transport)
4. Press "Start Navigation"
5. Follow the voice instructions

The app will automatically announce:
- When to turn left/right
- Bus numbers arriving at the stop
- Current stop name when you arrive
- Potential obstacles ahead

## Accessibility Tips
- Use headphones for better voice clarity
- Speak clearly when giving voice commands
- Keep the phone in your pocket — the app works in background mode
